MEXT Scholarship Guide for Nepalese Students

Complete Guide to Japanese Government Scholarship for Fully Funded Study in Japan

November 15, 2024 By Mirai Institute Team 12 min read Scholarship, Study Abroad, Funding

The MEXT (Ministry of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology) Scholarship is Japan's most prestigious scholarship program, offering Nepalese students fully-funded opportunities to study at top Japanese universities.

What MEXT Scholarship Covers

Round-trip Airfare

Economy class tickets from Nepal to Japan and back after completion

Full Tuition Coverage

100% tuition and examination fees at Japanese universities

Monthly Stipend

¥143,000-148,000 per month (depending on study level)

Accommodation Support

University dormitory or housing allowance

Research Allowance

Additional funding for thesis research and academic activities

15-20

Nepalese students receive MEXT scholarship each year

Eligibility Criteria for Nepalese Students

Academic Requirements
  • Undergraduate: Completed 12 years of schooling, under 25 years
  • Master's: Bachelor's degree (16 years education), under 35 years
  • PhD: Master's degree, under 40 years
  • Research Student: University degree, under 35 years
Academic Performance
  • Minimum GPA: 3.0/4.0 or 75% overall
  • No failing grades in final two years
  • Strong academic record in relevant field
Language Requirements
  • For Japanese-taught programs: JLPT N2 or higher
  • For English-taught programs: IELTS 6.0+ or TOEFL 79+
  • Japanese language training provided if needed
Health Requirements
  • Good physical and mental health
  • Medical certificate from approved hospital
  • No contagious diseases

Complete Application Timeline

April - May

Application announcement by Japanese Embassy in Kathmandu

June - July

Document submission deadline. Written exams at embassy.

August - September

Interviews at Japanese Embassy. Results announced.

October - December

University placement process. Acceptance letters sent.

January - March

Visa processing and pre-departure orientation.

April

Departure for Japan. Academic year begins.

Common MEXT Questions from Nepalese Students

Yes! MEXT Scholarship covers:
  • Full tuition and examination fees
  • Monthly living allowance (¥143,000-148,000)
  • Round-trip airfare
  • Accommodation (university dormitory)
  • Japanese language training (if needed)
  • Medical insurance

You can indicate preferred universities (up to 3) in your application. However, final placement is decided by MEXT based on:
  • Your academic background and research plan
  • University capacity and professor availability
  • Language requirements match
  • Overall competitiveness of your application

Written exams at Japanese Embassy in Kathmandu include:
  • English/Japanese: Language proficiency test
  • Mathematics: For science/engineering applicants
  • Subject Tests: Based on your field of study
  • Interview: 15-20 minutes with embassy panel

We provide comprehensive exam preparation in our MEXT training program.

Success Tips from Our MEXT Scholars
  • Start preparing 1-2 years in advance
  • Focus on academic excellence (GPA 3.5+)
  • Develop a compelling research proposal
  • Build strong relationships with professors for recommendations
  • Practice for embassy interview with mock sessions
  • Connect with previous MEXT scholars for guidance
MEXT Quick Facts
  • Stipend: ¥143,000-148,000/month
  • Duration: 2-5 years
  • Airfare: Round-trip covered
  • Tuition: 100% covered
  • Nepalese: 15-20 selected yearly
