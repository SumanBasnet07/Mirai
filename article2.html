Japan Work Visa Guide for Nepalese Professionals

Complete Guide to Working in Japan for Nepalese IT, Engineering & Healthcare Experts

Japan offers excellent career opportunities for skilled Nepalese professionals. With proper guidance and preparation, you can build a successful career in one of the world's leading economies.

Types of Work Visas for Nepalese

1. Engineer/Specialist in Humanities/International Services

For: IT professionals, engineers, translators, designers

Requirements: Bachelor's degree or 10+ years experience, JLPT N2 preferred

2. Skilled Labor Visa (特定技能 - Tokutei Ginou)

For: Construction, agriculture, nursing care, hospitality

Requirements: Skills test, basic Japanese (N4), industry experience

3. Intra-Company Transferee

For: Managers, executives transferred to Japanese branches

Requirements: 1+ year in parent company, managerial position

4. Highly Skilled Professional

For: Top talent with high salaries and qualifications

Requirements: Points-based system (70+ points), fast-track to PR

Complete Application Process

Step 1: Find a Job Offer

Secure employment with a Japanese company. We help connect Nepalese professionals with Japanese employers.

Step 2: Certificate of Eligibility (COE)

Employer applies for COE from Immigration Bureau in Japan (takes 1-3 months).

Step 3: Visa Application

Submit visa application at Japanese Embassy in Kathmandu with COE and required documents.

Step 4: Visa Issuance

Processing takes 5-7 working days. Single-entry visa valid for 3 months.

Step 5: Arrival in Japan

Convert to Residence Card at airport. Register address at local municipality within 14 days.

Required Documents for Nepalese Applicants

Essential Documents
  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Visa application form (download from embassy website)
  • Recent passport-size photographs (4.5x4.5cm)
  • Certificate of Eligibility (original)
  • Employment contract or offer letter
  • Company registration documents of employer
Educational Documents
  • Bachelor's/Master's degree certificates (attested)
  • Academic transcripts
  • Professional certificates (if applicable)
  • Japanese language certificates (JLPT preferred)
Additional Documents
  • Resume/CV (Japanese format recommended)
  • Work experience certificates
  • Bank statements (3-6 months)
  • Medical certificate (from approved hospitals)

High-Demand Jobs for Nepalese in Japan

IT & Software Development

Average Salary: ¥4,000,000 - ¥8,000,000 per year

Skills Needed: Programming (Java, Python, etc.), cloud computing, cybersecurity

Companies Hiring: Rakuten, LINE, Mercari, many startups

Engineering

Average Salary: ¥4,500,000 - ¥7,000,000 per year

Skills Needed: Mechanical, electrical, civil engineering expertise

Companies Hiring: Toyota, Honda, Sony, Panasonic

Healthcare & Nursing

Average Salary: ¥3,500,000 - ¥6,000,000 per year

Skills Needed: Nursing license, caregiving experience, JLPT N2

Opportunities: Hospitals, nursing homes, healthcare facilities

Salary Expectations & Cost of Living

Monthly Expenses in Tokyo
  • Rent (1 bedroom): ¥80,000 - ¥150,000
  • Utilities: ¥15,000 - ¥25,000
  • Food: ¥40,000 - ¥60,000
  • Transportation: ¥10,000 - ¥20,000
  • Insurance: ¥15,000 - ¥25,000
  • Total: ¥160,000 - ¥280,000
Average Monthly Salaries
  • Entry-level IT: ¥250,000 - ¥350,000
  • Mid-level Engineer: ¥350,000 - ¥450,000
  • Senior Developer: ¥500,000 - ¥700,000
  • Nurse: ¥300,000 - ¥400,000
Note: Salaries are higher in Tokyo but so is cost of living. Many Nepalese professionals save 30-50% of their salary.
